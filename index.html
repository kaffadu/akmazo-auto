Simple Process

How It Works

Getting your dream car from an international auction to your garage in Ghana is simpler than you think. Here's our proven process.

01
Choose Your Vehicle

Browse our inventory search or tell us your preferred make, model, year and budget. We'll find the perfect match.

02
We Bid & Win

Our experienced team bids strategically at Copart, IAA and other global auctions to secure the best price for you.

03
Shipping & Clearing

Vehicle is shipped via container to Tema Port. We handle all customs documentation, CEPS registration and duties.

04
Delivered to You

Your vehicle is delivered directly to you in Accra or any location in Ghana — fully documented and road-ready.
